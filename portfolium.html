Me, by myself:

My name is Bruno. Bruno is said to come from the old Teutonic language 'Brun' and mean something like 'brown'. I have 34 years of existence in this world. I think there are a few more years to go. I was born on a piece of land that they used to call Santa Catarina. But I never felt much from here. I'm more of a carioca sometimes, more of an Italian, some more of German, and maybe a bit of oriental. I'm an islander. Living on an island has its advantages. It seems that we belong to another world. This is good, sometimes. But in the event of a future sea level rise, it will be pretty bad.

  • Jiu jitsu: I am still a mere blue belt. But it's already something.
  • Languages: I speak the language of Shakespeare, Dante and I scratch the language of Goethe and Rabelais. Cervantes' too.
  • Programming: I'm still learning. But I think Python is cool.
